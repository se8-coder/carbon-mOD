Our Origin

Carbon mOD began as a bold experiment by the Lexa Engineering Group. While observing the massive plumes of soot from local industrial districts, our founders realized that what the world viewed as "waste" was actually an untapped reservoir of high-grade carbon material.

We spent two years developing a proprietary filtration mesh capable of surviving the extreme temperatures of industrial furnaces while capturing carbon particles at the molecular level. Today, Carbon mOD is a leader in circular emission technology, helping industries transition from "exhaust" to "asset."

The Mission

To accelerate the global transition to a carbon-neutral economy by turning industrial exhaust into a sustainable source of high-purity carbon materials. We aren't just cleaning the air; we are building the raw materials for the next century of infrastructure.

Molecular Carbon Capture (MCC) Technology

Our proprietary MCC system utilizes a multi-stage electrochemical process to isolate CO2 and particulate matter with extreme efficiency.
