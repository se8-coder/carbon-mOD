Advanced Carbon Recovery Lifecycle

01

Capture

Our proprietary reactor systems are installed at industrial exhaust ports, capturing up to 94% of particulate carbon before it enters the atmosphere.

02

Refine

The captured soot is processed through high-temperature molecular ovens, removing impurities and creating high-purity carbon black and graphite precursors.

03

Repurpose

The refined carbon is then distributed to manufacturing partners for use in carbon fiber, high-performance batteries, and sustainable construction materials.
